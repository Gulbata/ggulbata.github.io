Georgian Food

Georgian cuisine has a distinct character while bearing some similarities with various national cuisines of the Caucasus and wider Eastern Europe. Every region of Georgia has its own distinct style of food preparation. Because we want to show Georgia from a slightly different angle, we pay more attention to the modern urban character, both in food and design. In addition to Georgian food and wine, we would like to introduce you to our contemporary culture. We believe that Georgian modernism has a lot to tell and it is as interesting and exciting to discover, as it’s history and traditions are. That is exactly why one of our favorite artists, “Eternally Modern” Petre Ostkheli was chosen to represent our brand.
