Lendav Maaler

The name of our restaurant and the main motive of its concept is connected with the famous Georgian artist, set and costume designer, and one of the founders of Georgian modernism - Petre Otskheli. The inspiration for our logo was his iconic artwork created in 1937 for the movie "Winged Painter". So, another ‘Lendav’ landed in Tallinn. Why? Because we want to show Georgia from a slightly different angle, pay more attention to the modern urban character, both in food and design. In addition to Georgian food and wine, we would like to introduce you to our contemporary culture. We believe that Georgian modernism has a lot to tell and it is as interesting and exciting to discover, as it’s history and traditions are. That is exactly why one of our favorite artists, “Eternally Modern” Petre Ostkheli was chosen to represent our brand.
